Explanation of Project

It's a project to investigate a communication technology using a variety of resources. The origin, operating principle, uses, impacts, and possible future of the global positioning system. Adobe indesign was used to edit this infographic.


Working principles

The 4 satellites orbiting the earth sned signals and interpreted by a GPS device. A GPS device calculates its distance from each satellite, and then use geometry to determine its location on Earth in 3D.
